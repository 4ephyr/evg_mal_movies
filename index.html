List of Evergreen malayalam movies

1. Manichitrathazhu

List of cast

  1. Mohanlal
  2. Shobhana
  3. Suresh Gopi
  4. Revathy

Plot

The story is inspired by a tragedy that happened in an Ezhava tharavad of Alummoottil meda' a old (Traditional house) located at Muttom (near Haripad), a central Travancore Channar family, in the 19th century. The Story writer Madhu K Panicker or Madhu Muttam himself belong to this Alummoottil Tharavad.
